Minstagram Beta

I'm a visual artist who supports my studio working as a web developer. I'm tired of Instagram. I've had good and bad moments with it, but ultimately it's never really felt like a place designed for artists.

Minstagram is the working title for a free minimal Instagram alternative that I'd like to build during an upcoming batch at the Recurse Center in New York with the help of the experienced software engineers in their community.

[more on recurse center] [more info on me]

Core Vision...

Minstagram will be whatever it's artists want it to be. My hope is just that it will be a neutral space built for artists by artists where we can share work and connect with each-other. Some thoughts on that below.

  • chronological feed
  • no hidden algorithms
  • no paid advertisements
  • no toxic / addictive features
  • customizable user pages
  • no link restrictions
  • no paywall for artists
  • free and open source

100 Beta Users...

For no particular reason the beta group will be limited to 100 users who will be invited to optionally help guide the design of the platform. Anyone is welcome to apply but the target user groups are below.

  • practicing visual artists
  • gallerists / curators
  • potential collectors
  • art students
  • art lovers
