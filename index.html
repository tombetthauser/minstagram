Minstagram Beta

Hi there! I'm a visual artist who supports my studio working as a web developer. I'm burnt out on Instagram. I've had some good and bad moments with it, but ultimately it's never really felt like a place designed for artists.

Minstagram is the working title for a free minimal Instagram alternative that I'd like to build during an upcoming batch that I've been invited to attend at the Recurse Center based in Brooklyn.

[more on recurse center] [more info on me]

The Basic Idea...

Minstagram will be whatever artists want it to be. My hope is just that it turns into a neutral space where artists can share work and connect with each-other built for artists by artists. Some thoughts on what that looks like below.

  • minimal instagram alternative
  • built for artists by artists
  • simple chronological feed
  • customizable user pages
  • no hidden algorithms
  • no paid advertisements
  • no paywalls for artists
  • no link restrictions
  • no toxic features
  • free and open source

100 Beta Users...

To keep things simple the initial beta group will be limited to 100 users who will be invited to optionally help guide the design of the platform. Anyone is welcome to apply but the target user groups are below.

  • practicing visual artists
  • gallerists / curators
  • potential collectors
  • art students
  • art lovers

Other Thoughts...

This project has no delusions of competing directly with large for-profit platforms. But there's more than one kind of success and open source proofs of concepts are important for building a better web.

As long as their are artists interested in this using whatever this platform becomes I'll continue to freely maintain, promote and improve it.
